Pills Against Humanity

eCommerce interface design & design system

✺ Main roles
Research . Branding . Design system . Prototyping . Copywriting . Packaging

✺ Team
Melissa Brasil

✺ Time span
5 weeks (2022)

✺ The challenge
The challenge was to select a physical product, create and use a Design System, and design an online store that sells that product (desktop version only).

✺ The concept
As my physical product I chose to work with pills of intrinsically human characteristics (that, ironically, we lack so much). This concept arose from my constant desire to do original things with a purpose and that can help people reflect on issues that I consider important. I chose to do a minimalist packaging and website to focus on the societal critique.
