Rethinking the Fine Arts Faculty Website

website reformulation

✺ Main roles
Research . Information arquitecture . User testing . Data analysis

✺ Team
André Dinis . Mafalda Santos . Marta Aleixo . Melissa Brasil

✺ Time span
5 weeks (2022)

✺ The challenge
The challenge was to develop concrete solutions to improve the user experience on the website, according to the needs and frustrations of our segment (students).

✺ The approach
The process of choosing a programme, right up to the moment they start applying, can be complex and frustrating, so our aim was to make life easier for a candidate. We decomplexified and enriched important information, and improved the efficiency of the website through the restructuring of the information.
